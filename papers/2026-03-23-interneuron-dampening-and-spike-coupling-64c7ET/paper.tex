\begin{abstract}
This bundle presents a reproducible pipeline to quantify how acute reduction of interneuron activity affects spike--spike coupling and population synchrony. The package attempts to find public NWB datasets via DANDI and otherwise runs a synthetic example demonstrating expected outputs. The methods include unit classification, pairwise correlations, population coupling, GLM coupling filters, and spike--LFP spectral analyses.\end{abstract}
