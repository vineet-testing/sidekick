Research question: Sex differences in pediatric epilepsy for RNS patients

Search summary:
- Searched PubMed and PMC for pediatric responsive neurostimulation (RNS) and sex differences.
- Located pediatric RNS cohort studies and pooled analysis.
- Found one multicenter PMC full text with baseline sex composition (34% female; 12/35).
- Did not locate pediatric RNS studies that reported sex-stratified seizure outcomes or formal sex-difference analyses in the retrieved sources.

Conservative interpretation:
- Current retrieved pediatric RNS literature appears sparse on sex-specific analyses.
- Evidence supports describing representation and overall outcomes more readily than sex differences in outcomes.
